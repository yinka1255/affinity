          Description
          Sakura: brings you a culmination of the finest culinary delights from across Asia onto your plate. With Masterchefs who excel at cooking Asia's wide array of rich and wonderful food, Sakura offers masterpieces ranging from Teppanyaki and Sushi, to Tempura and Yaki Soba Noodles. We also offer many vegetable dishes for our Vegetarian friends with nearly all our quality ingredients imported from Europe and The Far East. Sakura looks forward to serving you soon. The Far East just got a little closer... Double Four: is one of the oldest Lebanese cuisine restaurants established in 1989. We serve a variety of oriental dishes, from Lebanese Mezze to premium barbecue dishes.In our family atmosphere you can dine in, get a take away or you can order a delivery.
